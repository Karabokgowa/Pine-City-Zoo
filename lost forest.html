LOST FOREST

Step into the Lost Forest and immerse yourself in an ancient woodland habitat. Wander along shaded trails, spot native wildlife, and enjoy the tranquility of towering trees and flowing streams.

Activities available:

  • Guided nature walks at 11h00am and 2h30pm
  • Bird watching stations
  • Picnic areas with scenic views

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Lost Forest lies at the northern edge of the zoo, near the reptile house.
