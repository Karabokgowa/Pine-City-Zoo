ANIMALS MAP PLACES

MONKEY TRAIL

Explore the winding paths of the Monkey Trail where you can observe a variety of playful monkeys in a lush, natural setting. Perfect for families and nature lovers looking for an interactive experience.

Highlights:

  • Watch capuchins swing and play
  • Learn about monkey behavior and conservation
  • Interactive feeding times at 10h30am and 3h00pm

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The Monkey Trail is located near the east entrance, past the Bird Sanctuary.
